  <h2>What is stored on your device</h2>

  <p>The app stores one flag locally on your device to remember whether you have completed the onboarding screens. This flag is stored using your device's standard preferences storage. It contains no personal information, is never transmitted anywhere, and is removed when you delete the app.</p>

  <p>All app content — the eight chemicals, twenty products, and five swaps — is bundled with the app at install time. The app makes no network requests during normal operation.</p>
