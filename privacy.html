Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 May 2026

Micro Detox is built and maintained by Navjeet Malli, an independent developer based in Australia.

What we collect

Nothing. Micro Detox does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data.

The app does not:

  • Require an account or sign-in
  • Track usage or behaviour
  • Send any data to any server (including ours)
  • Use analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting services
  • Use advertising identifiers or fingerprinting
  • Access your contacts, photos, location, microphone, or any other device sensor

What is stored on your device

The app stores one flag locally on your device to remember whether you have completed the onboarding screens. This flag is stored using your device's standard preferences storage. It contains no personal information, is never transmitted anywhere, and is removed when you delete the app.

All app content — the eight chemicals, twenty products, and five swaps — is bundled with the app at install time. The app makes no network requests during normal operation.

Children's privacy

Micro Detox is intended for adults. It is not directed at children under 13 and does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the change will appear in a new version of the app and the date above will be updated. There is no other way the policy can change because there is no server to update.
